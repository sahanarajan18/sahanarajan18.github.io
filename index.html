Hello! I am a doctoral research fellow in scientific metaphysics at University of Delhi (India). I research on methodological issues in scientific metaphysics, with focus on neuroscience and theories of mind/consciousness. Specifically, I endorse a deflationary approach to metaphysics and suggest the contextualisation of metaphysical issues within scientific theories.
