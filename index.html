Hello! I am a Lecturer at the Jindal Global Business School (JGBS) of O.P. Jindal Global University (JGU). I research on methodological issues in scientific metaphysics, with focus on scientific theories of consciousness. Specifically, I endorse a deflationary approach to metaphysics and suggest the contextualisation of metaphysical issues within scientific theories.
