Dissertation: Overview

Research Question
Ontic Structural Realism is one of the recent and rigorously developing metaphysical systems within the domain of scientific metaphysics.
  1. Objections to Ontic Structural Realism
  2. Possible Rejoinders to the Objections
Current Stage

Do entities and processes of fundamental physics invoke entities and processes of special sciences? I claim that they do.

I argue that certain metaphysical lessons from experimental data and results have been neglected in philosophy of science (especially, within Ontic Structural Realism). By considering them, we can understand that the entities and processes of special science constitute sets of conditions in which the entities and processes of fundamental physics exist within changed environments.I illustrate this through current hypothesis about formation of subatomic particles in early universe.

My objective, as a philosopher of science and metaphysician, is to bring back the environment to the centerstage of debates on primacy of physics and unity of sciences; here, I understand environment as a modal concept, as a set of conditions which makes something possible.
